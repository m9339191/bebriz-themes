## About Luxe Fashion

Luxe Fashion is a premium Shopify OS 2.0 theme designed specifically for fashion, apparel, and lifestyle stores. With over 50 built-in features, it provides everything you need to create a high-converting online store without relying on third-party apps.

## Why Choose Luxe Fashion?

Built on the latest Shopify Online Store 2.0 architecture, Luxe Fashion gives you full control over every page with drag-and-drop sections. The theme is meticulously optimized for performance, accessibility, and SEO — ensuring your store loads fast and ranks well.

### Designed for Fashion

Every element of Luxe Fashion has been crafted with fashion and lifestyle brands in mind. From color swatches that swap product images to lookbook sections with hotspot tagging, the theme understands how fashion shoppers browse and buy.

### No Apps Required

Unlike themes that depend on expensive third-party apps, Luxe Fashion includes wishlist, product comparison, quick view, AJAX cart, mega menu, newsletter popup, and dozens more features built right in. This means faster load times, fewer compatibility issues, and lower monthly costs.

### Mobile-First Approach

With over 70% of e-commerce traffic coming from mobile devices, Luxe Fashion is built mobile-first. Every interaction uses 44px touch targets, swipe gestures feel native, and the layout adapts beautifully across all screen sizes.

## Performance & Standards

- Lighthouse performance score optimized
- Semantic HTML5 markup throughout
- Structured data (JSON-LD) for rich search results
- WCAG accessibility guidelines followed
- RTL language support included
- Cross-browser tested (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
