Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase a theme, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your license key and purchase receipt
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe; we do not store card details
  • Order details — product purchased, date, and amount

When you contact us, we collect:

  • Name, email, and message content

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver purchased products and license keys
  • Provide customer support
  • Send product updates (for purchased themes only)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process your data:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Vercel — API hosting for purchase verification

4. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. Third-party services (Stripe) may set cookies during the checkout process.

5. Data Retention

Purchase records are retained for accounting and support purposes. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption and secure payment processing through Stripe.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at our contact page.
